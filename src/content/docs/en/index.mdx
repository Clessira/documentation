## In short

NowDoing asks you at a configurable interval what you just worked on. You tap the matching activity, the entry is logged, you keep working. No pomodoros, no project trees, no surveillance — just honest data about your day.

## What NowDoing is not

- **Not a surveillance tool.** NowDoing does not watch what you do — you tell it.
- **Not a cloud service.** No account, no server, no sync.
- **Not a subscription.** Free during beta, one-time purchase at release.
