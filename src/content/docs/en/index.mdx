## In short

Clessira asks you at a configurable interval what you just worked on. You tap the matching activity, the entry is logged, you keep working. No pomodoros, no project trees, no surveillance — just honest data about your day.

## What Clessira is not

- **Not a surveillance tool.** Clessira does not watch what you do — you tell it.
- **No cloud service of our own.** No account, no Clessira server, no telemetry. Optional sync runs only through your own private Apple iCloud.
- **Not a subscription.** Free during beta, one-time purchase at release.
