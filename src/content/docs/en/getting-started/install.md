## System requirements

- macOS **26.4** or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
- About 30 MB free disk space

## TestFlight (beta)

Clessira is currently in public beta — free, with all features.

1. Open the TestFlight invite link from [clessira.app](https://clessira.app).
2. Install the TestFlight app if you don't have it.
3. Tap **"Join"**, then **"Install"**.

After release, Clessira will be available in the Mac App Store as a **one-time purchase**, not a subscription.

## First launch

Clessira doesn't announce itself loudly: it appears in the menu bar and shows a small welcome wizard.

Permissions the app may request:

- **Notifications** — so the tracking prompt can appear as a macOS notification when the app is in the background.
- **Launch at login** — optional, so Clessira is available right after your Mac logs in.

What the app does **not** request: full disk access, screen recording, input monitoring. Clessira knows nothing about what you do — only what you tell it.
