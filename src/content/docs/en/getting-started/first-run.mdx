On first launch, Clessira opens an eight-step welcome wizard. Anything you pick here can be changed later in Settings. Use **< Back** to step back and **Continue** to move on.

## 1. Welcome

<Image src={step1} alt="Clessira welcome screen" />

Just a greeting — no setup yet. Click **Get started** to enter the wizard.

## 2. How often should Clessira ask?

<Image src={step2} alt="Tracking interval selection" />

Pick the rhythm at which Clessira should ask what you are working on. The default is **45 minutes** — a balance between accurate data and minimal interruption.

Available intervals: 5, 10, 15, 20, 30, **45**, 60 min.

Shorter intervals (10–15 min) give finer data, longer ones (30–60 min) interrupt less. More under [Tracking interval](/en/features/tracking-interval/).

## 3. Display & notification

<Image src={step3} alt="Prompt placement, menu bar display, and day view" />

Configure how the prompt appears and how the day is shown:

- **Prompt position** — *Popover* (anchored to the menu bar) or *Centered* (top center, next to the notch). See [Prompt display](/en/features/prompt-display/).
- **Menu bar display** — *Icon only* or *Icon + live text* with the current activity.
- **Day view** — *Grouped*, *List*, or *Timeline* as the default view inside the popover. See [Timeline & views](/en/features/timeline/).
- **Sound** — short cue when the prompt appears.
- **Break** — default length for manual breaks, adjustable in 5-min steps (5–120 min). See [Breaks](/en/features/breaks/).

## 4. When do you work?

<Image src={step4} alt="Weekdays, weekly target, and holiday mode" />

Active days feed into the daily target. Outside working hours, Clessira shows no prompts.

- **Weekdays** — tap the days you work.
- **Weekly target** — your target hours per week (0–80 h).
- **Respect public holidays** — no prompts on holidays. When enabled, pick your country for the matching holiday calendar.

Details under [Working hours & weekly target](/en/features/working-hours/).

## 5. Enable integrations

<Image src={step5} alt="Integrations: Spotlight and VS Code" />

Optional integrations — everything stays local on your Mac.

- **Index notes in Spotlight** — find activities and notes via macOS Spotlight. See [Spotlight](/en/integrations/spotlight/).
- **VS Code integration** — when you switch Git branches, Clessira automatically opens the prompt. Enabling it stores the security token encrypted in the macOS keychain. Approve the keychain prompt when asked. See [VS Code](/en/integrations/vscode/).

The hint about **Recurring activities** (standups, daily checks, meetings) is informational only — templates live later under **Settings → Management → Recurring**, see [Recurring activities](/en/features/recurring/).

## 6. Your data belongs to you

<Image src={step6} alt="Privacy overview and storage location" />

A summary of what Clessira does — and does not — do with your data:

- **Local by default** — everything lives in `~/Library/Application Support/Clessira`.
- **No tracking** — no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting.
- **VS Code integration optional** — communication runs strictly over `localhost`.
- **Erasable** — data can be deleted anytime in Settings.

**Change folder…** lets you pick a custom storage location — for example an external drive or a cloud folder. To sync between multiple Macs instead, use iCloud sync in Settings (see [Data & privacy](/en/reference/data-privacy/)) — it merges changes entry by entry. The default keeps the standard location.

## 7. Bring activities along?

<Image src={step7} alt="Import existing activities from Jira, Excel, Markdown, or plain text" />

If you already have a list of tickets or tasks, paste it in directly. Supported sources:

- **Jira tickets** — `PROJ-123 Summary` becomes an activity, the project becomes a group.
- **Excel / CSV** — tab-, comma-, or semicolon-separated with a header row.
- **Markdown lists** — bullets, numbered lists, nesting.
- **Plain text** — one activity per line.

Click **Import…** to paste a list. You can also skip this step and import later under **Settings → Management**. Details under [Import activities](/en/features/import/).

## 8. Finish & autostart

<Image src={step8} alt="Final step with autostart toggle" />

Clessira is set up. Before you launch:

- **Launch at login** — recommended, so the tracker is in the menu bar every time you log in.

Hit **Start**, and the wizard closes. Clessira now lives in the menu bar — click the icon to open the popover, or press <kbd>⌃⌥⌘L</kbd> to summon the prompt from anywhere.
