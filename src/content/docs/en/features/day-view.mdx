The popover shows the selected day in full depth: what you did and when, how far along you are toward today's target, and which activity is running. By default activities are grouped by group, and every row carries a colored progress bar.

1. **Date navigation.** Arrows step one day at a time. Clicking the date in the middle jumps back to **Today**. The calendar icon on the right opens a month picker for targeted jumps.
2. **Group header.** Each activity group shows its day total and a bar whose width reflects the share of the day total. The color is the group's color.
3. **Activity row.** Indented under the group, each activity is listed with its tracked duration. The bar below it shows the share of the **group total** (or, when ungrouped, of the day total). Hovering over the dot on the left reveals a play button that starts the activity as the current one — today only.
4. **Weekly goal indicator.** When an activity has a weekly goal, a small ring appears at the end of the row and fills as the week progresses. When you go over the goal, the ring turns orange and a warning triangle appears next to it — you've overrun the plan.
5. **Daily target.** The bar compares the time tracked today against your daily target (`actual / target`). Once you pass the target or the overtime thresholds configured in Settings, the bar turns orange or red.
6. **Day metrics.** From left to right: today's total, the start of the first entry, the end of the last entry, and the location icon. Clicking the icon toggles the day between office and home office; with a weekly office target set, the building fills proportionally to the days achieved.
7. **Control bar.** Recording toggle with the running activity's name, start or end a break, pin the popover (stays open even when you click outside), menu with Settings and Timesheet, and **+** to create a new activity or add an entry after the fact.

## Gaps in your day

When more than a few minutes go untracked between two entries, NowDoing shows a **Gaps** block under the last activity row with start, end and duration for each gap. NowDoing does not back-fill them — it's up to you whether to log them after the fact.

## Editing entries

Below the groups there's an **Entries** disclosure. Open it and every time block of the day is listed with start, end and duration; hovering a row reveals a trash icon to delete that entry.

For smaller corrections without opening the disclosure, every activity row carries a `±` field. It accepts inputs like `+10m`, `-30m` or `+1h30m` and shifts the duration accordingly.

## Opening other days

Use the arrows or the calendar to switch to past days. Past days are fully editable — the **+** button adds a retroactive entry for the day you're currently viewing.

## Other view modes

The view shown here is the **grouped** day view, the default. The popover offers two further variants of the same day — a horizontal timeline and a chronological list. Which view fits when is described in [Timeline & views](/en/features/timeline/).
