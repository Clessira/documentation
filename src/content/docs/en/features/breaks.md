---
title: Breaks
description: Pause manually, set the default duration — when Clessira skips entries.
---

Clessira knows two kinds of breaks: a **manual pause** you trigger, and a **default duration** that is pre-filled for that manual pause. Set the duration under **Settings → General → Break** or in the [welcome wizard](/en/getting-started/first-run/) at step 3.

## Manual pause

Right-click the menu bar icon → **"Pause"**.

While paused:

- **No prompt** is shown.
- **No new entries** are created.
- The running stopwatch stops.

End the pause via right-click → **"Start tracking"** — or it ends automatically after the configured default duration.

## Default duration

Anywhere from **5 to 120 minutes**, in 5-minute steps. Default: **30 minutes**.

The duration applies to the *manually triggered* pause. For longer breaks (lunch, an off-site meeting), just resume tracking yourself — or bump the default up.

## Breaks in the Timesheet

Breaks show up as a **gap** in the day — no entry, no activity name. In the [Timesheet](/en/features/timeline/) you'll see gaps in the *timeline* and *list* views; the *grouped* view does not count them.
