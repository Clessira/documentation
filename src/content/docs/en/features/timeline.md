title: Timeline & views
description: Three views on your day — Groups, Timeline, List — and when to use which.
---

NowDoing offers **three views** on today inside the popover. Switch between them with the tabs at the top.

## Groups

The **Groups** view is the default. It shows your activities as a list with day totals, sorted by duration.

```
🛠️  Coding         3:42
📞  Calls          1:15
✉️   Email          0:48
☕  Break          0:30
```

Quick check: "How much have I coded today?" — one click answers it.

## Timeline

The **Timeline** shows your day as a horizontal, color-coded bar — from morning to evening. Each block is an activity; width is duration.

**What is this good for?** At a glance you see:

- Where your focus block was.
- How often you context-switched.
- Where the gaps are (breaks, meetings, off-screen time).

Hover a block to see the name and time range.

## List

The **List** is the chronological, detail-precise view: every entry with start, end, and duration.

```
09:00 – 10:20  🛠️  Coding         1:20
10:20 – 10:45  📞  Calls          0:25
10:45 – 11:00  ☕  Break          0:15
11:00 – 12:30  🛠️  Coding         1:30
12:30 – 13:15  —   (Gap)          0:45
13:15 – ...    ✉️   Email          running
```

Gaps are **shown explicitly** — NowDoing does not back-fill them. So you keep visibility on what you actually tracked vs. what you didn't.

This view also lets you edit or delete individual entries directly.

## Timesheet (separate window)

For week views or multiple days, open the **Timesheet** — right-click the menu bar icon → **"Timesheet"**.

There you'll find:

- Day and week views
- Totals per activity and per day
- CSV export for day or week (see [CSV export](/en/features/csv-export/))
